Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is one of my favourite Western dishes. Since we started out with lasagna, I was trying to come up with other pastas that I liked, but I couldn't really come up with anything. Aglio Olio is a little to oily for me, and most pastas are unappealing as I usually just throw it in some water and wait for it to boil.

Carbonara however is rich in cheese, which I love slurping down. Pair it in a set meal with ice lemon tea, and it becomes even better, as the ice lemon tea offsets the heaviness of the cheese.
